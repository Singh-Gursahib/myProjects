Welcome To

Equation Engine

This website is dedicated to solving general matrix problems, but has the potential to solve a wide range of mathematical problems using advanced algorithms. This website was created as part of a project for the COMP2680 course at TRU in Kamloops, in the year 2023.

My aim is to provide an easy-to-use platform for solving complex mathematical problems, and I hope you find EquationEngine helpful in your endeavors.

How to use Equation Engine?

Want to become a matrix mastermind? Our video tutorial covers everything from matrix addition to finding roots using the quadratic formula. Get ready to impress with your math skills and become a pro at matrix operations. Check it out now!
